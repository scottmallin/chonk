Chonk is a system to help separate your site into clean, well-spaced chonks

Like this

<section class="chonk">
    <div class="container">
        <div class="row">
            <div class="col">
                Use with Bootstrap, Tailwind, or whatever. I'm not your boss.
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>

Chonks stack to keep whitespace under control

    <div class="container">

            <div class="col">
                Good friends stack together.
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>
